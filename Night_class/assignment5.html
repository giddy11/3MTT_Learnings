Featured Recipe: Italian Pasta Carbonara

Pasta Alla Carbonara

This creamy, savory pasta dish is a staple in Italian cuisine. It's made with just a few ingredients: pasta, pancetta, eggs, and Parmesan cheese, but it's bursting with flavor!

Ingredients

Pasta: 200 grams

Pancetta: 100 grams

Eggs: 2 large

Parmesan Cheese: 50 grams

Black Pepper: Freshly ground

Salt: To taste


Instructions

Step 1: Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Cook the pancetta in a pan until it becomes crispy.

Step 3: Whisk the eggs and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.

Step 4: Add the pasta to the pancetta, remove from heat, and immediately mix in the egg mixture.

Step 5: Toss well to ensure the pasta is coated in the creamy sauce. Season with black pepper and serve hot.

Chef's Tip

Important: Be sure to mix the eggs off the heat to avoid scrambling them. This is what gives the Carbonara its creamy texture!
