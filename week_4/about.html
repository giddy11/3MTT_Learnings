WHO WE ARE

Pan-African Institute Of Professional Studies is an institute of innovative excellence with global recognition for delivery quality training and learning services. It’s a school with a focus on developing professionals and young graduates in various fields. Our learning development focuses on – Quality Training Delivery – Research And Development – Technological Advancement – Multicultural Environment.

Our programmes promote high levels of independence through innovative learning and assessment interventions. Students can expect a carefully integrated mix of practical lectures; access to well-designed self-study materials and online learning resources.

WHY CHOOSE US

Learning takes place for everyone. Our safe and caring educational environment offers many personalized and unique opportunities. Students explore the world beyond school, learning about the industry opportunities, careers, work, and leadership.Students participate in self-directed, trainer-guided instruction while contact with an advisor and peer advisee group provides a tailored guidance experience towards successful certification.

We have designed an enhanced instruction program as we foster a dynamic, supportive learning environment. Our curriculum is unique—designed to adapt to the changing and diverse needs of the global industries and to prepare our students to compete both academically and in the job market.
